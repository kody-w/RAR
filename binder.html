How it works

Your binder is stored in your browser (IndexedDB). It persists across sessions and works offline.

Export downloads a binder.json file you can back up, share, or import into another browser.

Import merges cards from a binder.json file into your collection without duplicates.

To sync globally, push your exported binder.json to your RAPP-compliant repo and enable GitHub Pages.
